Frequently asked questions

Where does the event data come from?

Tournament, athlete, and team data is aggregated from public IBJJF and AGF sources and refreshed daily. Because the underlying sites sometimes change structure, there may be occasional gaps — please report anything that looks wrong.

Why don't I see brackets for a tournament?

Brackets are fetched live from the tournament organizer's public bracket system. They typically appear 24–72 hours before the event and disappear shortly after. If a tournament has started but brackets aren't loading, the organizer's system may be down — try again in a few minutes.

How do I set a home city for distance filtering?

Open the Settings tab → Home City, type a city and tap Save. The Events tab will then let you filter by distance (50 / 100 / 250 / 500 mi).

Does the App work offline?

Previously loaded data remains viewable, but fetching new events or brackets requires an internet connection.

How do I delete my data?

All data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the App removes everything. See the Privacy Policy for details.

Is the App free?

Yes. No ads, no subscriptions, no in-app purchases.

Who builds this?
